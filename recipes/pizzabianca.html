Grilled Pizza Bianca

Tasty Pizza Bianca

Prepared pizza dough allows the cook to grill a tasty pizza bianca, or white pizza, in under 30 minutes.

Why call out for delivery when you can make an incredible pizza at home?

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 tablespoon
  • Garlic: 1 clove, minced
  • Heavy Cream: 1/3 cup
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1 cup freshly grated and divided
  • Pizza Dough: 1 pound
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 8 fresh slices

Steps

  1. Melt butter: Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds; stir in cream. Add 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese, and stir until smooth. Let Alfredo sauce cool to room temperature, about 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat gas grill: Preheat a gas grill with all burners on High for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Divide pizza dough: Divide pizza dough in half. Shape each portion of the pizza dough into an 8 to 10-inch disc. Reduce the flame on half the grill burners to Low, to create areas of direct and indirect heat.
  4. Place discs of dough onto grill: Carefully place discs of dough, one at a time, onto the grill over direct heat. Close the cover. Let dough grill until the bottom is just barely cooked and shows char marks, 1 to 3 minutes.
  5. Turn crust ninety degrees: Turn crust ninety degrees (do not flip dough over). Grill until crust shows light grill marks, 1 to 3 minutes.
  6. Move pizza dough to indirect heat: Spoon Alfredo sauce over dough. Top each crust with four slices mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle remaining 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese evenly over pizzas.
  7. Grill until cheese is melted: Grill until cheese is melted, about 3 minutes. Carefully remove pizza from the grill.
  8. Preheat the ovens broiler: Broil pizza until cheese is golden, about 5 minutes. Allow pizza to rest a few minutes before cutting into wedges.
